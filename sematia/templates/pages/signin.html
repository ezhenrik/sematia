Sematia is an online platform for creating linguistic layers from EpiDoc TEI XML documents, using different subsets of XML-encoded textual variants. It is also an open source repository for linguistic annotations of the layers.

By uploading content to Sematia, you agree to release it under a Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 License (cc-by).

Sematia is hosted by the University of Helsinki IT Services.
